Admissions

Admission Process

Raisina Bengali School welcomes applications from students seeking quality education in a nurturing environment. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and fair, ensuring that deserving students get the opportunity to be a part of our esteemed institution.

The admission process typically begins in January each year for the upcoming academic session. Applications are accepted for all classes subject to the availability of seats.

Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility criteria for admission varies depending on the class. Here are the general guidelines:

  • For Nursery: Child should be at least 3 years old as of March 31st of the admission year.
  • For KG: Child should be at least 4 years old as of March 31st of the admission year.
  • For Class 1: Child should be at least 5 years old as of March 31st of the admission year.
  • For other classes: Successful completion of the previous class from a recognized school.

Required Documents

The following documents are required at the time of admission:

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate of the child
  • Transfer certificate from the previous school (for classes II and above)
  • Report card of the previous class
  • Passport-sized photographs of the child and parents
  • Address proof
  • Aadhar card of the child (if available)

Fee Structure

The fee structure for the current academic year is available at the school office. You can also download the fee structure from our website or contact the admission office for detailed information.
