About Us

Our History

Raisina Bengali School is committed to excellence in education, holistic development, and nurturing young minds into responsible citizens. Founded with a vision to provide quality education with a focus on Bengali culture and heritage, our school has been serving the community for decades.

The school was established in the year 1925 and has since been a beacon of educational excellence. Our institution has a rich history of academic achievements and cultural contributions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where students can develop their intellectual, physical, emotional, and social potential. We aim to instill values of respect, responsibility, and resilience in our students.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that prepares students to be global citizens with strong cultural roots and values. We envision our students as future leaders who will contribute positively to society and uphold the rich cultural heritage of Bengal.
