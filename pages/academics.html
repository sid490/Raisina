Academics

Curriculum

Raisina Bengali School follows the CBSE curriculum, which is designed to provide a holistic education that focuses on the overall development of students. Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning.

We offer a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Languages, Computer Science, and various co-curricular activities to ensure a well-rounded education.

Academic Excellence

Our school has a long-standing tradition of academic excellence. Our students consistently perform well in board examinations and competitive tests. We provide a supportive learning environment where students are encouraged to strive for excellence.

Our dedicated faculty members are committed to providing quality education and personalized attention to each student. We use innovative teaching methodologies to make learning engaging and effective.

Facilities

We have state-of-the-art facilities to support academic learning and co-curricular activities:

  • Well-equipped science laboratories
  • Modern computer labs with internet access
  • Comprehensive library with a vast collection of books
  • Smart classrooms with interactive teaching aids
  • Sports facilities for various indoor and outdoor games
  • Art and music rooms for creative expression
